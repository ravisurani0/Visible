How does your engagement model work?

We are flexible. We can work on a project basis, a monthly retainer for ongoing support, or hourly for specific tasks. We adapt to what works best for your business.

What is your typical response time?

We aim to respond to all support requests within 24 hours. For critical issues, we offer expedited support options.

Can you work with our existing code?

Yes, that is our specialty. We are experienced in taking over and maintaining existing codebases, even if they are older or poorly documented.

Do you offer ongoing maintenance?

Yes, we offer monthly maintenance packages that include security updates, backups, and minor content changes to keep your site running smoothly.

We offer both hourly billing and fixed-price quotes for defined projects. We will always provide an estimate before starting any work.

We accept all major credit cards, bank transfers, and PayPal. Invoices are sent electronically with easy payment links.
